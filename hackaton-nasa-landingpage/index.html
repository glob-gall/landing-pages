BOIA

More than 25 million tons are discarded into the sea annually and 80% of this waste is plastic, and poses many risks to marine fauna.

Analyze in real time the data collected by our buoys

The great importance of this project lies in the contribution provided by the rich information made available to help with the biggest problems of tracking, quantifying and locating plastic debris in the ocean. Thus, marine fauna, quality of life in coastal communities, navigation safety may be less affected by these problems.

Using IOT and AI, researchers can keep tracking the direction of trash movement through low-orbiting satellites. With BOIA, it would be more accessible to analyze and predict the behavior of plastic in the oceans for eventual control and removal.

Goals

The goal of the BOIA project is to map and analyze data on waste in the oceans, providing data and analysis to governments and NGOs with information received from buoys scattered across the open sea.
